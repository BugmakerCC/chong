About Me

My name is Chong Chen. I am a master’s student at the School of Software Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University. My supervisors are Dr. Jiachi Chen and Dr. Yanlin Wang. Currently, I am interning at The State Key Laboratory of Blockchain and Data Security, Zhejiang University.

My research mainly focuses on: (1) LLM4SE; (2) Program Analysis, with a primary focus on the applications of LLMs in code quality and the Web3 domain.
